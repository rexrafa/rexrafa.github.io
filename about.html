About Me

Welcome to my blog! I'm Rex Rafa, a passionate Cloud Solutions Architect and DevOps Engineer with extensive experience in AWS, automation, and artificial intelligence. I share my knowledge and insights through this blog, posting twice a week about the latest trends and best practices in cloud computing, DevOps methodologies, and AI technologies.

My journey in technology has led me to work with various organizations, helping them transform their infrastructure, implement robust DevOps practices, and leverage the power of cloud computing. I believe in continuous learning and sharing knowledge with the community.

When I'm not architecting cloud solutions or writing code, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing insights on LinkedIn about the evolving landscape of cloud computing and artificial intelligence.
